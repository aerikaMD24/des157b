3 Years... 100 Poems

Welcome, readers!

As a child, I'd been in love with words. From playing with wordplay to assonace to my absolute favorite: rhymes. I'd written poems basically all my life.

Yet something must have really hit me in my teen years because from ages 14-16 I found myself writing dozens of poem! For 3 years (2016-2018), I'd somehow managed to write 100 poems.

That's a lot, I know. So here's a little visualizer to help you imagine that!
